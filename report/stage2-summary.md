## Notes

- `Stage1 Samples` are counted from `analysis/<STUDY>/stage1/<STUDY>.fam`.
- `Stage2 Samples` are counted from the sample columns in the imputed VCF header.
- `ID Style` is inferred from sampled final VCF IDs and reports `rsID`, `chr:pos:REF:ALT`, or `mixed`.
- `Qual Tag` is the INFO field used for imputation quality, prioritising `R2`, then `INFO`, `DR2`, and `ER2`.
- `Chr Files` expects the chromosomes present in the stage-1 `.bim`; studies with no chrX in stage 1 are assessed on autosomes only.
- `Indexed` counts `.tbi` files present beside the final imputed VCFs.
- `Variants` are summed across the final imputed stage-2 VCFs.
- Per-chromosome counts are read from `analysis/<STUDY>/stage2/report/tables/*imputation_metrics.tsv` when present; missing or threshold-mismatched metrics fall back to a direct VCF scan.
- `>= 0.3` counts variants meeting the pipeline-quality threshold.
- `>= 0.8` counts variants in a higher-confidence quality tier.
- `Mean Qual` is the mean imputation-quality value across scored variants.
- `Empty Chrs` flags any output VCF with zero variant records.
- `Missing Chrs` flags chromosomes with no final imputed VCF.
- `Sample Match` should stay `yes`; imputation should not change the sample count.
